  2665→## 🤖 Claude (02-28 01:21)
  2666→
  2667→看到问题了。range JOIN 太重是因为 30M × 93K 范围匹配，SQLite 扛不住。
  2668→
  2669→黑丝转 Python 二分查找方向是对的，但有个更简单的方案可以告诉他：
  2670→
  2671→**直接 JOIN 两次 `regime_timeline`，不需要 segments CTE：**
  2672→
  2673→```sql
  2674→SELECT cs.strategy, cs.coin, cs.interval,
  2675→    rt_open.regime,
  2676→    COUNT(*) AS trade_count,
  2677→    ROUND(AVG(pt.r_value), 4) AS avg_r,
  2678→    ROUND(100.0 * SUM(CASE WHEN pt.pnl > 0 THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) / COUNT(*), 1) AS win_rate
  2679→FROM paired_trades pt
  2680→JOIN combo_summary cs ON pt.combo_id = cs.id
  2681→JOIN regime_timeline rt_open
  2682→    ON cs.coin = rt_open.coin
  2683→    AND cs.interval = rt_open.interval
  2684→    AND pt.open_candle_idx = rt_open.candle_idx
  2685→JOIN regime_timeline rt_close
  2686→    ON cs.coin = rt_close.coin
  2687→    AND cs.interval = rt_close.interval
  2688→    AND pt.close_candle_idx = rt_close.candle_idx
  2689→WHERE rt_open.regime = rt_close.regime
  2690→  AND rt_open.regime IN ('bull', 'bear', 'ranging')
  2691→GROUP BY cs.strategy, cs.coin, cs.interval, rt_open.regime
  2692→HAVING trade_count >= 20
  2693→ORDER BY rt_open.regime, avg_r DESC;
  2694→```
  2695→
  2696→**为什么更快：** `regime_timeline` 有 `UNIQUE INDEX (coin, interval, candle_idx)`，两次 JOIN 都是点查询 O(1)，不是范围扫描。
